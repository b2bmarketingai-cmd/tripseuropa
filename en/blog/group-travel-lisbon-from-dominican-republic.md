## Visa Requirements for Dominican Republic Travelers

Citizens of the Dominican Republic need a **Schengen visa** to enter Portugal. Since Portugal is part of the Schengen Area, a single Schengen visa allows you to travel across 27 European countries, making it excellent value if your group plans to explore beyond Lisbon.

### Key Schengen Visa Tips for Dominican Travelers

- Apply at least **6 to 8 weeks** before your departure date
- Required documents include a valid passport, travel insurance, proof of accommodation, bank statements, and a detailed itinerary
- Group visa applications can sometimes be processed together, streamlining the paperwork
- The visa fee is approximately **80 EUR per person**
